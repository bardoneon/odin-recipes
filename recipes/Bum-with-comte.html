Bum with Comte

One of the staples of the Danish morning, have it on the go while cycling or in any of Copenhagen's expensive coffee shops

Ingredients

  • Freshly baked Bum (from tomorrow, if possible)
  • 10 gm. of whipped butter
  • 3 slices of Comte cheese

Steps

  1. Place the ingredients on a plate
  2. Take a nice picture, and don't forget to upload it to instagram!
  3. Enjoy!
